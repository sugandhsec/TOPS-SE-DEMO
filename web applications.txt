WEB APPLICATIONS:   Web apps are designed to provide users with interactive experiences and can perform a wide range of functions, such as allowing users to create and edit documents, manage data, collaborate with others, or access various online services.


Real world Example : 

	Online Shopping: A web application that allows customers to browse products, add items to their cart, and complete the checkout process.

Social Networking: A web application that enables users to create a profile, connect with others, and share content such as photos, videos, and messages.

Banking: A web application that allows customers to view their account balances, transfer funds, pay bills, and perform other banking tasks online.
